Privacy & Security Dark Mode HELP Privacy & Security Overview

This Mobile Inventory Management System helps staff view, add, update and delete inventory records. This page explains how the app looks after privacy and security for this data.

Data We Store

This app stores inventory data only, including:

  • Item ID (auto-generated)
  • Item name
  • Category (Electronics, Furniture, Clothing, Tools, Miscellaneous)
  • Quantity and price
  • Supplier name
  • Stock status (In Stock, Low Stock, Out of Stock)
  • Featured item flag
  • Special note (optional)

The app does not collect customer details, payment information, or other sensitive personal data.
Only authorised users can change inventory data.
All changes are sent over a secure connection.

How We Protect Your Data
  • Inventory data is sent between the app and server using HTTPS (encrypted connection).
  • Only logged-in staff with access to the inventory system can add, update or delete items.
  • The app only uses this data to manage stock and does not share it with third parties.
  • No personal customer or payment details are stored in the app or inventory database.
